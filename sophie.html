You Were Cool by the Mountain Goats

You Were Cool is an unreleased song by the Mountain Goats that I didn't discover until several years as a fan. When thinking of songs to play for people, this one was a clear favorite for you. I don't know how hard high school was for you—you always sounded encircled by friends—but I think the sentiment is a bit universal.

You can view my cover by clicking on this text.


If you want to see the one and only John Darnielle play it, I can recommend these two performances, but there are others to be found.

  • Carnegie Hall
  • The Newport Folk Festival
